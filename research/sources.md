## Topic

## Selection Criteria
Experts were shortlisted using these filters:
1. Direct relevance to B2B SaaS, GTM, product marketing, demand generation, revenue strategy, or adjacent operating roles.
2. Practical thinking with reusable insight, not generic motivational content.
3. Clear evidence of strategic judgment on positioning, pipeline, demand generation, growth execution, or content systems.
4. Content quality strong enough to support a future playbook.

The goal is not to collect the biggest names, but to identify practitioners whose content contains reusable insight for a real B2B SaaS LinkedIn organic strategy project.
